Welcome To Sahyog Dairy

We're a Leader in the Dairy Market

Cooperation is a quality that resides in the spirit of every community member. Our union believes in shared prosperity — bringing together thousands of farmer families under one roof to produce, process, and deliver quality dairy across the region.

What began as a handful of village-level milk societies has grown into one of the most trusted dairy cooperative brands, built on the pillars of trust, transparency, and community-first values.

Know More About Our Cooperative

Our founding chairman cocooned a dream to uplift the socio-economic status of village farmers, which was fulfilled through the creation of a cooperative milk union — modeled after the ideal cooperative dairy of its era. What started with a handful of village-level milk societies collecting and pouring milk has since grown into a network spanning multiple states.

— Main Processing Plant
